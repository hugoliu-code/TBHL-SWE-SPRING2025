AI Stretching Instructor

Goal: 
  To create an application that can be a helpful tool to students as they try to built better habits and behaviours that with contribute to them living a healtier lifestyle.

Description:
  This is a mobile application that will take care of all your stretching need. Whether you just finished going to the gym or you're about to go play a sport, our AI Stretching Instructor will suggest stretches  
  based on the physical activity you will be or have just finished engaging in. Complete with a profile to save past workouts and stretch rountines, with each stretch having all the tools you need to replicate the    stretch.
